What is Wild WarZ?

We introduce a card-skill game that has a sustainable ecosystem with a rising incentives scheme that will sustain on its own.

  • The community will be the decision-maker in the ecosystem, making the ecosystem decentralized to the core.
  • Wild Wars is a card shedding game that requires high skill to eliminate all cards in hand. Played within a group of four, one person with the highest point wins the game.
  • A game may take several hands to conclude the winner.

NFT - Companion

  • In order to play Wild Wars, players will need to own a Wild NFT Companion through purchase or delegation.
  • While owning the exclusive Wild NFT Companion, one will be allotted with energy to play the game and complete daily tasks.

Players can earn by :

  • Completing daily tasks in-game.
  • Competing for the daily leaderboard and increasing points based incentive.
  • Players can also delegate their NFTs to other players for a passive revenue share.
  • Minting New NFT Companion and selling them on the secondary marketplace.
  • Staking Wild token for rewards. Wild token is the governance token of the game. The governance token also gives players the right to take part in important decisions for the future of the game.
